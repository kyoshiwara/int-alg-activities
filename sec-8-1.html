Subsection 8.1.1 Check Your Understanding

  1. In part (e) of Activity 1, how many terms of the product are quadratic? How many of the terms are cubic?

  2. Explain the difference between the expressions \((a+b)^3\) and \(a^3+b^3\text{.}\)

  3. Compare the formulas for expanding \((a-b)^2\) and \((a-b)^3\text{.}\)

  4. In Activity 3, why is the polynomial for the volume of the box cubic? What are the units of volume in this application?

Subsection 8.1.2 Wrap-Up

In this Lesson, we worked on the following skills and goals related to polynomials:

  • Compute products of polynomials

  • Compute cubes of binomials

  • Factor the sum or difference of cubes

  • Use polynomials as models
