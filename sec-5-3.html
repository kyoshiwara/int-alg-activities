Subsection 5.3.1 Check Your Understanding

  1. In Activity 1, describe the differences between the graphs of \(y=x^2\) and \(y=x^3\text{.}\)

  2. What connection do you see between the graphs of \(y=x^2\) and \(y=\sqrt{x}\text{,}\) and between the graphs of \(y=x^3\) and \(y=\sqrt[3]{x}\) ?

  3. Explain how we use asymptotes to help us sketch a graph.
