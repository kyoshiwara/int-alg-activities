Activity 9.3.4. The General Quadratic Equation.

The general quadratic equation in two variables is

\begin{equation*} Ax^2 + Bxy + Cy^2 + Dx + Ey + F = 0 \end{equation*}

We will only consider quadratic equations in which \(B=0\text{.}\) The graphs of these equations are conic sections.
