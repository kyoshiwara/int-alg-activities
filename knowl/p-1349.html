After introducing exponents of the form \(1/n\text{,}\) you might want to go over some of the Checkpoints in the text as a skills warm-up. Emphasize the difference between negative exponents and fractional exponents. Show students how to evaluate \(n\)th roots on their calculators using both radicals and exponents. You may choose to have students use technology to make the tables and graphs in the Activities.
