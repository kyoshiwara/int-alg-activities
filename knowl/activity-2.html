Complete the table of values showing the concentration, \(C\text{,}\) of toxic chemicals \(t\) years after 2000. Then write your answers as ordered pairs.

(b)

  1. Label the axes, plot the ordered pairs on the grid, and connect them with a straight line.

  2. In one or two complete sentences, explain why you should stop the graph at the horizontal axis.

(c)

Interpret the point \((16,45)\) for this situation. (Use complete sentences!)

(d)

Use the graph to find those years when the concentration will be below 120 ppm but above 30 ppm. Show your work on the graph.

(e)

Write an equation for the concentration, \(C\text{,}\) of toxic chemicals \(t\) years after 2000.

(f)

Write and solve an inequality to verify your answer to part (d).
