Activity 7.5.2. Compound Interest.

If \(P\) dollars is invested at an annual interest rate \(r\) (expressed as a decimal) compounded \(n\) times yearly, the balance of the account \(A\) after \(t\) years is given by

\begin{equation*} A = P \left( 1 + \dfrac{r}{n} \right)^{nt} \end{equation*}
