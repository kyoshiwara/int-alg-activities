Of course, students will now confuse the rules for simplifying complex fractions with the rules for adding fractions. It is a good idea to compare the two procedures on similar examples, say, adding \(\dfrac{2}{x} + \dfrac{1}{x-2}\) and simplifying \(\dfrac{1+\dfrac{1}{x-2}}{1-\dfrac{2}{x}}\text{.}\)
