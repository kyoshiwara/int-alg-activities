This Lesson extends the elimination method to 3x3 linear systems. We concentrate on the process of Gaussian elimination, because this useful technique generalizes to higher order linear systems. Students usually have no trouble mastering back-substitution in a triangular system. Although there is a lot of grumbling initially at the number of steps in Gaussian reduction, if you show students how to keep their work organized, after the second example they feel more comfortable.
