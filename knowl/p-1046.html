In this lesson we review cube roots and absolute value in preparation for studying the graphs of eight basic functions. When considering the two rational functions, we introduce the notions of vertical and horizontal asymptotes. Note that these topics should be treated intuitively and numerically; in particular we do not explicitly discuss limits. Nor do we use the terminology of domain and range, as these are topics for precalculus. At this stage, students still think of functions and graphs pointwise, and will only gradually progress to a more global view.
