\(3\) \(P(3) = \hphantom{00000}\)
\(4\) \(P(4) = \hphantom{00000}\)
\(5\) \(P(5) = \hphantom{00000}\)

(b)

Plot the data points from the table and connect them with a smooth curve.

(c)

Write a function that gives the population of the colony at any time, \(t\text{,}\) in days.
