Small animals like bats cannot survive for long without eating. The weight \(W\) of a typical vampire bat decreases over time until its next meal, according to the function

\begin{equation*} W = f(h) = 130.25 \times 10^{-0.126} \end{equation*}

where \(h\) is the number of hours since the bat's most recent meal, and \(W\) is in grams.
