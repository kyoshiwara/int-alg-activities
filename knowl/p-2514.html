In this lesson we consider hyperbolas and categorize the conic sections. You might want to start class with a calculator demonstration: solve \(x^2 + y^2 = 1\) for \(y\) and graph in a squre window, then do the same for \(y^2 - x^2 = 1\) and \(x^2 - y^2 = 1\text{.}\) If your class is strong enough, you can come back to these equations and graphs when you discuss the asymptotes of a hyperbola.
