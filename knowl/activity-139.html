Activity 9.1.3. Altitude of a Triangle.

(a)

Sketch the triangle with vertices \(A(-6,-3),~B(-6,3),~\) and \(~C(4,5)\text{.}\)

(b)

Find the slope of the side \(\overline{AC}\text{.}\)

(c)

Sketch the altitude from point \(B\) to side \(\overline{AC}\text{.}\) Find the slope of the altitude.

(d)

Find an equation for the line that includes the altitude from point \(B\) to side \(\overline{AC}\text{.}\) (Hint: You know a point \((x,y)\) that lies on the line.)
