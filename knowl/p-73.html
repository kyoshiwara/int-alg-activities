Look back at your work in Activity 2. Use the graph to show that the following three statements are different ways of saying the same thing:

  1. The point \((-1,7)\) lies on the graph of \(y=4-3x\text{.}\)

  2. \(x=-1,~y=7\) is a solution of the equation \(y=4-3x\text{.}\)

  3. \(x=-1\) is a solution of the equation \(7=4-3x\text{.}\)
