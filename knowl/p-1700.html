In this Lesson we introduce the formal definition of exponential function. Explain to students why we make the restrictions \(b \gt 0\) and \(b \not= 1\text{;}\) trying to evaluate \(f(x) = (-4)^{1/2}\) is a good example. Point out that the input values can be positive or negative, but ouptut values are always positive. Have students graph several examples on their calculators.
