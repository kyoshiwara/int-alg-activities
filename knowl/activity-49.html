Activity 4.2.3. Maximum or Minimum value.

A farmer plans to fence a rectangular grazing area along a river using 300 yards of fence.

(a)

Draw a sketch illustrating the grazing area, and label its length and width.

(b)

Let \(w\) stand for the width of the rectangle, and write an expression for the length \(l\) in terms of the width.

(c)

Write an equation for the area \(A\) of the grazing land in terms of \(w\text{.}\)

(d)

Graph the equation.

(e)

Use algebra to answer the questions:

  1. What is the largest area the farmer can enclose?

  2. What are the dimensions of the largest area?
