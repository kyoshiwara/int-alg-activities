Here is our first encounter with asymptotes. It may be helpful to graph a few translations of \(y=\dfrac{1}{x}\) and \(y=\dfrac{1}{x^2}\) with technology, e.g. \(y=\dfrac{1}{(x-2}\) and \(y=\dfrac{1}{x^2}+3\text{,}\) to show that asymptotes do not always coincide with the axes.
