This Activities Workbook provides a Lesson for each section in the book, consisting of Activities for students to complete in groups or with guidance from the instructor; or they can be used as support for a lecture format. The Lessons are designed to show students how the mathematical techniques they are learning can be applied to study and understand new situations. Each Lesson ends with a Wrap-Up and a set of "Check Your Understanding" questions for discussion.
