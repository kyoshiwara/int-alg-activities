Task 1.4.2.a.

Note.

To help students with part (a), ask: if 1/8 mile costs $0.30, how much would 1 mile cost? You can also point out that dividing $0.30 by 1/8 mile gives the same result as multiplying $0.30 by 8.

Compute the charge per mile in each city. (Do not include the initial charge.) In which city do taxis charge the highest mileage rate?
